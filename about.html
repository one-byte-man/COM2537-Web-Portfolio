Who Am I?

The Story

Ahmet Akpınar is a Computer Engineering student at Ankara University and an independent game developer driven by the philosophy that every byte counts.

His passion for game development was ignited in middle school when a teacher introduced him to Scratch, opening the door to a new world of possibilities. However, during the pandemic, his resources were limited to a Samsung S3 Mini. Lacking a computer, he spent hours watching game development tutorials, mentally coding and designing mechanics he could not yet build. Recognizing that passion required a strong technical foundation, he paused his development dreams to focus entirely on his academic goals.

His resilience was tested during the February 6th Earthquakes while studying at a Science High School. Despite the challenging circumstances, he transferred to an Anatolian High School and achieved a significant academic milestone, ranking 9,981st in Turkey in the university entrance exam (YKS) with a score of 515. This achievement paved the way to Ankara University.

After completing the English preparatory program, he acquired his first computer in November. He immediately began transforming years of theoretical knowledge into practice, mastering C# and Unity to release his first mobile game, 'Fruit Rain'. Today, publishing under the name 'One Byte Man', he continues to develop games for both PC and mobile platforms with a vision to establish his own development studio.
